Chapel Council Corner

By Ellen Le ('30).

After transitioning right from the Can Drive in November (with 99.33% participation from the Middle School in total!), the week before winter break had the MS Chapel Council and the Upper School Vestry buzzing with activity. The well-loved Scrooge Chapel took place on the last Monday before break, the space filling with laughter and fun. In the same week, the traditional Lessons and Carols Chapel had students in Special Dress for a more professional service, with hymns being sung and musical benedictions being offered. And even after the EA Community eases into break, the Chapel will still offer an annual Christmas Eve Eucharist on December 24th. The holidays are really never a dull time!
