Spirit Week

Spirit Week at our school was filled with excitement, creativity, and community pride. Each day brought a new theme, from Pajama Day to Class Color Day, and the halls were alive with laughter and school spirit.

Students competed in friendly contests, decorated hallways, and showcased their unique styles. The highlight of the week was the pep rally, where students and teachers joined together to celebrate school unity.

Spirit Week reminded everyone that our school isn’t just a place for learning — it’s a community where students express themselves, cheer for one another, and build lasting memories.
