How To Make a Reindeer Ornament

Materials:

3 Clothespins

Hot glue gun and glue

Markers (Red, Black, Green, Yellow/Gold)

Mini pom poms (red, white)

Small googly eyes

Steps:

Hot glue two clothespins together to make the reindeer’s legs and let them dry.

Once dry, glue the third one on, pointing up instead of down. This will make the head and antlers.

Next, glue the red pom pom at the bottom of the third clothespin to make the nose. Draw eyes with the black marker above the nose or glue to small googly eyes on.

Glue the white pom pom at the back of your reindeer to make the tail.

Finally, decorate the reindeer as you would like with the markers!
